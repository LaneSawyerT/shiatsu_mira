WHAT IS SHIATSU?:

  This form of Japanese bodywork works with the body’s energy lines and stimulates the body’s flow of energy working with the acupuncture meridians. It works in a non-invasive, respectful, and compassionate way. It supports an individual’s self- healing ability and increases the body’s awareness. It is a preventative therapy which helps maintain an individual’s vitality and stamina, helping to strengthen the vital organs and prevent energy from getting blocked in the body. It can be an excellent support for a person suffering from an incurable disease or a chronic illness. The focus is on the person and not the disease, enabling the person to control or bring relief to the disease.



  Shiatsu Therapy works well with mental and emotional problems and can help the person understand why and how they feel the way they do. Whether the problem is a new one, has been there for months or even years; Shiatsu Therapy can help. After an operation or in case of addictions(smoking, alcohol, drugs) Shiatsu Therapy can restore the energy and promote recovery. Shiatsu helps people to know themselves better by revealing the deepest tensions that relate to the psyche. While also, preventing different effects of stress and decreasing stress and anxiety that is already present. It works to rebalance the emotional and psychological situation in the patient.



  When receiving a Shiatsu treatment it means to regain the calm, and the dynamism, the sleep, and the capacity of recovery. It combats migraines and headaches, while simultaneously relaxing muscle contractions and reliving joint problems. For women it can be very useful during pregnancy or helping with menstrual issues. A Shiatsu treatment can be effective to help mobility, back pain, shoulder or neck tensions and limbs. Another benefit is that it will help stimulate the hormonal and immune system and increases the vigorous and the reproductive vitality. Shiatsu can also help regularise the metabolism. Also, one’s body posture will significantly improve. Shiatsu increases awareness of your own body while restoring the body’s energy balance. It is suitable for all ages and recommend for anyone that would like to improve or preserve their health.
