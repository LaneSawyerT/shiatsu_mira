WHAT IS SHIATSU?

  This form of Japanese technic literally means finger pressure. The pressure, along the acupuncture meridians, stimulates the body’s flow of energy helping to strengthen the vital organs and preventing the energy from getting blocked in the body, which is often just the result of a stressful event we experienced. It supports the individual’s self- healing ability and increases the body’s awareness. It can be an excellent support for a person suffering from a chronic illness or a condition that doesn’t seem to have any tangible cause. The focus is on the person and not the disease, enabling the person to control or bring relief to the disease.


  Receiving Shiatsu Therapy means to regain the calm, and the dynamism, the sleep, and the capacity of recovery. It combats migraines and headaches, while simultaneously relaxing muscle contractions and reliving joint problems. A Shiatsu treatment can be effective to help mobility, back pain, shoulder or neck tensions and limbs. Shiatsu stimulates the immune system and help regularise the metabolism. For women it can be very useful during pregnancy and post-partum recovery. The body posture will significantly improve. It is suitable for all ages and recommend for anyone that would like to improve or preserve their health.




  Shiatsu Therapy works well with psychological and emotional conditions and can help the person understand why and how they feel the way they do. Whether the problem is a new one, has been there for months or even years; Shiatsu Therapy can help.

Shiatsu treatment cycle can also help you identify triggers of anxiety, and help reduce the symptoms and causes. “What’s happening to my body?” “What and why has it changed?” “What is this tension on the right side of my neck linked to?” “What is this heaviness in my legs?” “Why do I grind my teeth at night?” And the list continues…
