Some benefits

Shiatsu can be used for a wide range of internal, musculoskeletal and emotional conditions. The techniques are used to relax the tension in the muscles and joints and to realign the body structure and has a strong influence on the nervous system. The Shiatsu treatment generally gives the receiver a sensation of beneficial relaxation, but also foster inner development and a thorough knowledge of energy processes. Shiatsu acts as a prevention, it helps to maintain the state of health and can be an effective support in the course of other therapies.
