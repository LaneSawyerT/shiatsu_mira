What is Shiatsu?

Shiatsu is a non-invasive therapy that helps reduce stress and contribute to overall wellbeing. Some benefits

Shiatsu can be used for a wide range of internal, musculoskeletal and emotional conditions.
The techniques are used to relax the tension in the muscles and joints. To realign the body structure. It improves the health of the immune and nervous system, aiming to balance the function of our organs. Shiatsu is most effective when used regularly as a preventative treatment. The sessions educate the receiver on what combinations are needed to lead a health-conscious lifestyle.
