About Me

  Hello! My name is Mira Marioni and I am trained as a Shiatsu Therapist and graduated from the International School of Shiatsu, Microbiotic Philosophy, in Italy with a Master's degree in 2011 and I am also a part of the Italian Shiatsu Federation.


   The connection between emotions and thoughts has always intrigued me so I have been researched the connection between these two, and how they effect our physical health from a very young age. Then, I attended a short course in my teenage years, and when I saw the effects of studying the basics I decided to progress on to completing my Masters over a four year period in Shiatsu.


  Regularly, I attend courses as often as time allows. I've attended workshops internationally as well. This all acts to fuel my personal development, as I am striving to improve as time goes on. I am very thankful to be in a position where my passion has become my profession.
